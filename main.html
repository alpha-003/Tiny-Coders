Access to medical records

You can keep track of your medical history and health records in one place, without carrying physical documents

Access to healthcare

You can use your ABHA number to search for registered health facilities and practitioners. You can also consult doctors across India, which can save time and money

Access to healthcare schemes

You can link all your health and insurance schemes to your ABHA number, making it easier to access them.

Security

ABHA uses encryption technology to secure your details. You must give your consent before sharing your information with any healthcare facility or professional.

Flexibility

You can deactivate your ABHA health card whenever you want to leave the platform

Informed decision-making

Doctors can use the information from your ABHA to make informed decisions about your prescription.
